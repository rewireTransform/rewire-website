Frequently Asked Questions

What does Rewire Consulting do?

Rewire Consulting helps small and mid-sized businesses eliminate operational drag and make smarter decisions through process optimization, workflow automation, business intelligence dashboards, and AI technology consulting.

What industries does Rewire Consulting serve?

We work with professional services, e-commerce operators, real estate teams, and SMBs with 10 to 100 employees who need better systems and data visibility to grow.

What tools and technologies does Rewire Consulting use?

We use Make.com, n8n, Python, and OpenAI APIs for automation; Power BI, Looker Studio, and Google Sheets for dashboards; and QuickBooks, Xero, and various CRMs for financial intelligence integrations.

How does Rewire Consulting charge for its services?

We offer fixed-scope project engagements starting with a Business Diagnostic, through to full Rewire engagements covering automation, dashboards, and AI workflows. Monthly retainers are available for ongoing optimization support.
